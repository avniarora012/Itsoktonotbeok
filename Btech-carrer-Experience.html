What does your day look like?

Umm about my day.. it actually varies a lot based upon a lot of factors, but here is how my typical day looks like. I am not a night owl, so i prefer waking up and starting my day early. First thing i do after breakfast is attempting leetcode POTD. If i have early morning class, i rush to it, else i solve some dsa/cp problems in the first half of my day. In second half, if there is no class or lab or any assignment pending, i either solve dsa/cp problem or work on my running project or learn technology needed for my project. In evening, i prefer going on a walk with my friends. At night either i chill or study for sem exam (during mid and end sems) or give contest (if there is any cf or lc contest scheduled) or do my pending work.

One thing no one tells you about this career?

Before coming to college, people made kinda misconception that once you get into iit/nit, you'll for sure get a good job. But only after actually spending some time in college i realised it's not that simple, you have to study properly, maintain decent cgpa, get good skills, in order to end up getting a good job. One more thing which i realised lately is, getting a first good job is not only about skills and cgpa, luck matters a lot more than we think.
